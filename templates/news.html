I'm not sure why I hadn't done this earlier. Previously, I simply showed off updates through Twitter, but obviously, not everyone has Twitter. Any new updates will go here along with a short Tweet telling people to check the News page to see the changelog.

Other than the news page, this update fixes a bug that caused certain cookies to expire just around 9 hours after their creation.

Despite making MSPA To Go months ago and using it to read then, I don't believe I had ever actually extensively used the Save/Load Game features (which were affected by this bug) until very recently. My school has those shitty Yondr pouches this year, and I am confined to using just the school Chromebook, so I've been reading Problem Sleuth using MSPA To Go while bored in class. At first, I thought the bug was the shitty school-enrolled Chromebooks wiping their cookies after a while, but using other sites made me realize it wasn't that.

So now we are here. I have fixed this incredibly inconvenient bug that slipped past me all these months, simply because I had assumed that JavaScript date functions used seconds and not milliseconds.
