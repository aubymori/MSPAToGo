MSPA To Go is a project made by an independent developer, not associated with Andrew Hussie, MS Paint Adventures, What Pumpkin, VIZ Media, or the Homestuck Independent Creative Union (HICU). It acts as a proxy to the official MS Paint Adventures (www.mspaintadventures.com), requesting assets from MS Paint Adventures, transforming them as needed, then sending them to the user. No MSPA comic content is stored permanently on the MSPA To Go server.

Some assets taken from MSPA are stored on the server. These include:

  • Images and audio used for the pageframe (some of which are modified).
  • The JavaScript code and images used for the fireflies from pages 009000 to 009024 and pages 009289 to 009303.
